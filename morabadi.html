⚠️ High Risk Area: Morabadi has been identified as a high-crime area. Extra caution is advised, especially for women and during night hours.

Area Overview

Morabadi is a densely populated area in Ranchi with a mix of residential and commercial establishments. The area has consistently reported higher crime rates, particularly incidents of harassment, assault, and theft. Poor lighting and crowded conditions contribute to the security challenges.

Crime Patterns

  • Harassment: High incidents of street harassment and eve-teasing
  • Assault: Physical assault cases reported frequently
  • Theft: Pickpocketing and snatching incidents common
  • Time Pattern: Peak incidents during evening hours (6 PM - 10 PM)
  • Hotspots: Market areas, bus stops, and narrow lanes

Safety Challenges

The area faces multiple safety challenges including inadequate street lighting, overcrowded public spaces, limited police presence during peak hours, and narrow lanes that provide easy escape routes for criminals. The high population density also makes it difficult to monitor all areas effectively.

Police Response

Local police have increased patrolling in the area and established additional checkpoints. However, the vast area and limited resources continue to pose challenges. Community policing initiatives are being implemented to improve safety.
